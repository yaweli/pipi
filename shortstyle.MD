## Short style


```HTML
<div ||T0/L20/BR6||>Hello</div>
```

Each shortcut represent a style. T for top:0px , L for left:20px , etc


|---------------|-------------------------|--------------|
|shortcut       | style                   | automatic px |
|T  | top | yes|
|T  | top | yes|
|T  | top | yes|
